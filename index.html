Material Handling Solutions

Vishwai Industries India Pvt. Ltd. provides customized material handling solutions to optimize efficiency and productivity. Our range includes trolleys, pallets, racks, conveyors, and more, meeting diverse needs.

We pride ourselves on our fabrication capabilities, delivering high-quality, durable products that adhere to stringent industry standards. Our in-house dunnage manufacturing facility allows us to provide precision-engineered solutions that are tailored to our clients' specific needs.

Our commitment to customer satisfaction extends beyond the initial installation.

Consultation and Support

Vishwai Industries India Pvt. Ltd. offers expert consultation and support services to ensure our clients' material handling needs are met efficiently.

Our team provides guidance on product selection, layout planning, and system design, tailored to your specific requirements. We also offer ongoing support to ensure the smooth operation of your material handling systems.

Turnkey Solutions

At Vishwai Industries India Pvt. Ltd., we are committed to delivering turnkey solutions that exceed our clients' expectations. Contact us today to learn more about how we can help you streamline your material handling processes and improve your overall efficiency.

Turnkey solutions include ongoing support and maintenance services to ensure that your material handling systems continue to operate smoothly. Our team of experts is dedicated to providing comprehensive assistance throughout the entire lifecycle of your system, from initial design to regular maintenance and upgrades.

Fabrication Services

We specialize in fabricating a wide range of products, including trolleys, pallets, racks, conveyors, plastic crates, scissor lifts, hand pallets, manual, and battery-operated stackers. Whether you need standard products or custom solutions, we can tailor our fabrication services to meet your specific requirements.

Our fabrication process is guided by strict quality control measures to ensure that every product meets our clients' expectations. We use only the highest quality materials and employ advanced fabrication techniques to ensure the durability and reliability of our products.

Maintenance and Repair

Our maintenance services include regular inspections, preventive maintenance, and repairs. We work closely with our clients to develop customized maintenance plans that meet their specific needs and budget.

Vishwai Industries India Pvt. Ltd. provides comprehensive maintenance and repair services to ensure that your material handling equipment operates efficiently and reliably. Our team of skilled technicians is trained to identify and resolve issues quickly, minimizing downtime and maximizing productivity.

Returnable Packaging

At Vishwai Industries India Pvt. Ltd., we specialize in Returnable Packaging solutions, designed to provide an efficient, cost-effective, and sustainable method for transporting and storing goods. Our Returnable Packaging products are constructed from high-quality, durable materials that can withstand repeated use, ensuring long-term reliability and reducing the need for single-use packaging. This not only helps in lowering costs but also significantly reduces environmental impact by minimizing waste.

Vishwai Industries' Returnable Packaging is customizable to meet specific requirements, ensuring that each solution is tailored to fit the unique needs of our clients. By investing in our returnable packaging solutions, companies can achieve significant savings in packaging costs, improve operational efficiency, and contribute to environmental sustainability
